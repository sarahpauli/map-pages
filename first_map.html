A Map of Florida's Biggest Cities

The four largest cities in Florida are, unsurprisingly, Jacksonville, Miami, Tampa, and Orlando, respectively. Jacksonville has the largest population, at approximately 985,843. Miami follows at 455,924, then Tampa at 403,364. Finally, Orlando is the fourth largest city in Florida, with a population of 320,742.
